    • Kia Ora!

    I'm a Jem Gallego, a web & software developer based in San Francisco. Breathing life to awesome ideas, yours and mine, is my driving passion. I also love making pretty things. When I'm not dabbling in design or code, I'm likely lurking on Twitter for the latest on my Warriors and 49ers or posting my latest creation from the kitchen on Instagram.

    I am currently available for both freelance work and a full/part-time position.

    I've always loved solving problems. As a kid, I often found myself glued to logic books and all kinds of puzzles. I am naturally intrigued by I like to make sense of things. In search of reading material on a trip to the East coast a couple of years back, I happen upon a book of Sudoku.

    I graduated from the California State University Long Beach with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science and a minor in Web Technologies and Applications.
