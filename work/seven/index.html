    • Ada Lovelace

    Art, Print

    I was commisioned by the CSULB ACM-W club to design a shirt for one of their events. They wanted a design centered around Ada Lovelace, considered to be the world's first computer programmer.

    I came up with a design using the binary digits, 1 and 0, to illustrate a portrait of Ada Lovelace as well as the ACM-W logo.
