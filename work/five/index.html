    • Joan Karissa

    Web, Design, Code

    Joan Karissa is a printmaker whose work has been featured on several exhibitions including Printing the Bay Area (Art Works Downtown, San Rafael) and PSBN 2014 (University of Hawaii, Hilo). Her work consists mostly of natures and city themes, many of which features places and landmarks in the Bay Area.

    I designed a site for her that is clean and simple in order to help showcase her amazing works of art free of distraction.
